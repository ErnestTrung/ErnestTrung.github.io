Avec L'Oncle Ho is a collection of anecdotes and stories the author had collected about the great Vietnamese revolutionist and leader Ho Chi Minh. 

The first story of the book retold in detail the life of Ho Chi Minh before, during and after the revolutionary wars against the American empire and the French colonists. The story also occasionally contains anecdotes told by his acquaintances. The following stories delved deeper into his childhood and his early adulthood. You can truly only understand the full extent of the hardships he had gone through by reading a biography like this book, just reading a summary on Wikipedia isn't enough.
